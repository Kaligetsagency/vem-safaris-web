Our Safari Vehicles

Your Custom-Built Home in the Bush

A successful and comfortable safari relies heavily on the quality of the vehicle. At VEM Safaris, we exclusively use specially modified, extended 4x4 Toyota Land Cruisers. These rugged vehicles are engineered to handle the harsh African terrain while providing maximum comfort and unbeatable wildlife viewing opportunities for our clients.

The Ultimate Viewing Experience

Photography and wildlife observation are the main reasons you come to Tanzania. Our vehicles are designed with this in mind.

  • ✓ Pop-Up Roofs: Every vehicle is equipped with a custom pop-up roof. This allows you to stand up safely during game drives for 360-degree, unobstructed views and perfect photography angles.
  • ✓ Guaranteed Window Seats: We limit the number of passengers per vehicle (typically a maximum of 6) so that every single guest has a window seat and plenty of space to stretch out.
  • ✓ Large Sliding Windows: Massive windows provide excellent visibility even when you are seated, and allow the cool breeze to flow through the cabin.

Comfort & Convenience

Long days in the bush demand a high level of comfort. Our Land Cruisers are essentially mobile lounges.

  • ✓ Onboard Charging Ports: Never miss a shot because of a dead battery. Our vehicles feature 220V and USB charging ports so you can charge your cameras and phones while we drive.
  • ✓ Mini-Fridge: Each vehicle comes with an onboard mini-refrigerator or high-quality cooler to keep your complimentary bottled water and soft drinks ice-cold all day long.
  • ✓ Comfortable Suspensions: Upgraded, heavy-duty suspension systems smooth out the bumpy, corrugated dirt roads of the national parks.

Safety First

Your safety is our absolute priority. Our fleet is meticulously maintained in our Arusha workshop after every single safari.

  • ✓ 4x4 Capabilities: True 4-wheel drive with differential locks to navigate deep mud, river crossings, and steep inclines with ease.
  • ✓ VHF Radios: Our guides are always connected via two-way long-range radios to share wildlife sightings with other drivers and call for assistance if needed.
  • ✓ Safety Gear: Every vehicle carries a comprehensive first aid kit, fire extinguishers, and two spare tires.
