8-Day Lemosho Route

The Most Scenic Path to the Roof of Africa

Trip Overview

The Lemosho Route is widely considered the most beautiful trail up Mount Kilimanjaro. Approaching from the west, it crosses the spectacular Shira Plateau before joining the Machame Route. The 8-day itinerary provides an excellent acclimatization profile, giving you the highest possible chance of successfully and safely reaching Uhuru Peak.

Day 1: Londorossi Gate to Mti Mkubwa (Big Tree Camp)

We drive from Arusha to Londorossi Gate for registration, then continue to the Lemosho trailhead. The trek begins through pristine, untouched rainforest where you might spot colobus monkeys, arriving at Mti Mkubwa Camp for your first night on the mountain.

Day 2: Mti Mkubwa to Shira 1 Camp

We leave the rainforest behind and enter the heather and moorland zone. The trail climbs steeply, crossing several streams, before opening up onto the beautiful Shira Plateau with expansive views.

Day 3: Shira 1 Camp to Shira 2 Camp

A relatively gentle day of trekking across the plateau to Shira 2 Camp. This day is crucial for acclimatization and offers stunning views of Kibo Peak floating above the clouds.

Day 4: Shira 2 Camp to Lava Tower to Barranco Camp

We follow the "walk high, sleep low" rule today. We ascend to the Lava Tower (4,600m) for lunch and acclimatization, then descend into the spectacular Barranco Valley, surrounded by towering cliffs and giant groundsels.

Day 5: Barranco Camp to Karanga Camp

The day starts with a fun, non-technical scramble up the famous Barranco Wall. Afterward, the trail undulates through alpine deserts and valleys before ending at the short, steep climb into Karanga Camp.

Day 6: Karanga Camp to Barafu Camp (Base Camp)

A short but steep trek takes us to Barafu Camp, our desolate, rocky base camp for the summit push. We spend the afternoon resting, hydrating, and preparing our gear. We go to sleep immediately after an early dinner.

Day 7: Summit Day! (Barafu to Uhuru Peak to Mweka Camp)

We wake up at midnight and begin the grueling, freezing climb up steep scree. We reach Stella Point at sunrise, then push on to Uhuru Peak (5,895m). After celebrating at the summit, we begin the long descent all the way down to Mweka Camp.

Day 8: Mweka Camp to Mweka Gate to Arusha

A final, beautiful descent through the lush rainforest brings us to the Mweka Gate, where successful climbers receive their certificates. We will drive you back to Arusha for a hot shower, a cold beer, and a celebration of your massive achievement.

Why Choose Lemosho?

  • ✓ High Success Rate: 8 days gives your body the necessary time to adjust to the extreme altitude.
  • ✓ Less Crowded Start: The western approach is much quieter than Machame or Marangu.
  • ✓ Incredible Scenery: Crossing the Shira Plateau offers unmatched panoramic views.
