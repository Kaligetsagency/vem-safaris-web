6-Day Cultural Safari

Tribes, Traditions, and Wildlife

Trip Overview

Go beyond the standard game drives and truly connect with the soul of Tanzania. This unique 6-day itinerary combines the thrill of spotting the Big Five in world-famous national parks with authentic, respectful visits to some of East Africa's most fascinating indigenous tribes, including the iconic Maasai and the ancient Hadzabe hunter-gatherers.

Day 1: Arusha to Tarangire National Park

Your journey begins with a drive to Tarangire National Park. Enjoy a full day of game viewing amongst the ancient baobab trees and massive elephant herds. In the late afternoon, we drive to the vibrant town of Mto wa Mbu for your overnight stay.

Day 2: Mto wa Mbu Walking Tour & Maasai Boma

Spend the morning on a guided walking tour through the farming village of Mto wa Mbu, tasting local banana beer and seeing traditional wood carvers at work. After lunch, we visit an authentic Maasai Boma to learn about their semi-nomadic lifestyle, jumping dances, and cattle-herding traditions.

Day 3: Lake Eyasi - The Hadzabe & Datoga Tribes

We wake up before dawn and drive to the rugged Lake Eyasi region to meet the Hadzabe, one of the last true hunter-gatherer tribes on Earth. You will have the incredibly rare opportunity to join the men on a morning hunt using bows and arrows. Later, we visit the neighboring Datoga tribe, renowned for their skilled blacksmithing.

Day 4: Ngorongoro Crater Descent

After a cultural immersion, we return to the wildlife. We drive up into the Ngorongoro Highlands and descend 600 meters into the crater floor. Enjoy a full day tracking lions, hippos, and the endangered Black Rhino in this breathtaking natural amphitheater.

Day 5: Lake Manyara National Park

We spend the day exploring the lush, groundwater forests and alkaline shores of Lake Manyara National Park. Keep your eyes peeled for the park's famous tree-climbing lions and massive troops of baboons. We overnight in a lodge overlooking the Rift Valley.

Day 6: Return to Arusha

Enjoy a relaxed morning breakfast with panoramic views of the escarpment before we make the scenic drive back to Arusha. We will drop you off at your hotel or the airport for your onward journey, bringing your cultural and wildlife adventure to a close.
