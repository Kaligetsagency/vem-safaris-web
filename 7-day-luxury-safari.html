7-Day Luxury Safari

The Ultimate Tanzanian Experience

Trip Overview

Experience the raw beauty of the African bush without sacrificing a single comfort. This premium 7-day itinerary combines private, world-class guiding with stays at Tanzania's most exclusive boutique lodges and luxury tented camps. From sipping champagne on the rim of the Ngorongoro Crater to flying over the endless plains of the Serengeti, this is the safari of a lifetime.

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Upon arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport, your private chauffeur will transfer you to a luxury coffee lodge hidden within a working coffee plantation. Relax by the pool, recover from your flight, and enjoy a gourmet welcome dinner.

Day 2: Tarangire National Park

Your private guide will collect you in a customized 4x4 Land Cruiser. We drive to Tarangire, famous for its giant baobab trees and massive elephant herds. Tonight, you will stay in a luxury lodge featuring private viewing decks overlooking the Tarangire River.

Day 3: Lake Manyara to Ngorongoro Crater Rim

Enjoy a morning game drive in Lake Manyara, searching for tree-climbing lions. In the afternoon, we ascend the Great Rift Valley escarpment to the rim of the Ngorongoro Crater. Your luxury suite offers breathtaking, uninterrupted views of the caldera below.

Day 4: Ngorongoro Crater Descent & Fly to Serengeti

We descend into the crater at dawn for a highly rewarding game drive in this wildlife haven. After a premium picnic lunch, we drive to the local airstrip and take a scenic bush flight directly into the heart of the Serengeti, maximizing your time and avoiding long, dusty drives.

Day 5 & 6: The Endless Plains of the Serengeti

Spend two full days exploring the Serengeti from your exclusive luxury tented camp. These canvas pavilions offer king-sized beds, en-suite bathrooms, and private butler service. Enjoy sundowner cocktails by the campfire as you listen to the distant roar of lions.

Day 7: Serengeti to Arusha (Departure)

After a final early morning game drive and a lavish bush breakfast, you will board a scheduled flight from the Serengeti back to Arusha. We will provide a day room for you to shower and repack before transferring you to the airport for your journey home.
