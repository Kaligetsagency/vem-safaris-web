10-Day Family Adventure

Wildlife, Culture, and Zanzibar Beach

Trip Overview

Designed specifically for families with children, this itinerary perfectly balances thrilling wildlife encounters with relaxation. We have minimized long drive times, selected child-friendly lodges with swimming pools, and included a flight to Zanzibar for some well-deserved beach time at the end of your adventure.

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Welcome to Tanzania! Your guide will meet your family at the airport and transfer you to a beautiful lodge just outside Arusha. The kids can stretch their legs in the gardens or jump in the pool while you recover from your international flight.

Day 2: Tarangire National Park

A short drive brings you to Tarangire, famous for its massive elephants. Kids love seeing the "Tree of Life" (giant baobabs) and watching baby elephants play in the river. We arrive at your lodge by mid-afternoon so the family can relax.

Day 3: Lake Manyara & Maasai Village

We do a morning game drive in Lake Manyara looking for baboons, flamingos, and tree-climbing lions. In the afternoon, we visit a local Maasai village. Your children will love seeing how Maasai kids live, learning to make fire with sticks, and watching the traditional jumping dance.

Day 4: Ngorongoro Crater Descent

We wake up early to descend into the "Eighth Wonder of the World." Because the wildlife is so concentrated, the kids won't have to wait long to see the action. We'll have a picnic lunch by the hippo pool before ascending and driving to the Serengeti border.

Day 5 & 6: The Serengeti Plains

Spend two days exploring the world's most famous national park. Our expert guides know how to keep children engaged with "wildlife bingo" and tracking lessons. We stay in a secure, family-friendly tented camp where you can hear the sounds of the African bush at night.

Day 7: Fly from Serengeti to Zanzibar

To avoid a long, dusty drive back to Arusha, your family will board a bush plane right in the Serengeti and fly directly to the tropical island of Zanzibar! A driver will take you to a beautiful beachfront resort.

Day 8 & 9: Zanzibar Beach Relaxation

Two full days of fun in the sun. Build sandcastles on the white-sand beaches, swim in the turquoise Indian Ocean, or take an optional boat tour to see dolphins and snorkel among colorful coral reefs.

Day 10: Stone Town & Departure

We head into historic Stone Town for a quick, engaging walking tour through the narrow alleys before transferring your family to the Zanzibar Airport for your flight home.

The Family Advantage

  • ✓ Pacing: Slower mornings and afternoons off at the lodge pool to prevent "safari fatigue."
  • ✓ Direct Flights: Flying from the Serengeti to Zanzibar saves 12+ hours of driving.
  • ✓ Engaging Guides: Our guides are fantastic with kids and love teaching them about the bush.
