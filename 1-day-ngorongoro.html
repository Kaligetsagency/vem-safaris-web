1-Day Ngorongoro Tour

The Ultimate Day Trip from Arusha

Trip Overview

Only have a single day to spare? Make it count. The Ngorongoro Crater offers the highest density of wildlife in Africa, meaning you can experience a complete safari in just a few hours. This fast-paced day trip takes you from Arusha straight to the crater floor for an unforgettable wildlife encounter.

Early Morning: Arusha to Ngorongoro

Your guide will pick you up at your hotel in Arusha at 6:00 AM sharp. We embark on a scenic 3-hour drive through the Great Rift Valley, passing through the farming town of Karatu before arriving at the Ngorongoro Conservation Area gate. Enjoy the spectacular panoramic views from the crater rim before we begin our descent.

Mid-Day: Crater Floor Game Drive & Picnic

We descend 600 meters into the caldera. The crater floor is a natural enclosure for over 25,000 large mammals. We will spend the core of the day tracking prides of lions, massive elephant bulls, herds of buffalo, and keeping a sharp lookout for the critically endangered Black Rhino. Around 1:00 PM, we will stop at the Ngoitokok Hippo Pool for a scenic picnic lunch right inside the park.

Late Afternoon: Ascent & Return to Arusha

After a final loop around the crater's alkaline lake to spot flamingos, we begin our steep ascent up the crater wall in the late afternoon. We will drive back to Arusha, dropping you off at your hotel or the airport around 6:00 PM in time for your evening flight or dinner.
