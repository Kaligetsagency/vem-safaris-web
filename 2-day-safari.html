2-Day Short Safari

Tarangire & Ngorongoro Crater

Trip Overview

Short on time but still want to experience the magic of the African bush? This compact, action-packed 2-day safari takes you to two of Tanzania's most famous parks. You will marvel at the giant elephant herds of Tarangire and descend into the world-famous Ngorongoro Crater for a chance to spot the Big Five.

Day 1: Arusha to Tarangire National Park

After an early breakfast in Arusha, your VEM Safaris guide will pick you up and drive you to Tarangire National Park (approx. 2 hours). Known as the "Home of the Giants," you will spend the full day game driving among ancient baobab trees and massive elephant herds. In the late afternoon, we will exit the park and drive to the town of Karatu, located in the Ngorongoro Highlands, for dinner and your overnight stay.

Day 2: Ngorongoro Crater Descent & Return

We wake up very early, grab our packed breakfasts, and drive to the Ngorongoro Conservation Area gate. We will descend 600 meters into the crater floor as the sun rises. The crater is teeming with wildlife, and you have an excellent chance of spotting lions, hyenas, hippos, and the rare Black Rhino. After a picnic lunch by the hippo pool, we will ascend the crater wall and drive back to Arusha, dropping you off at your hotel or the airport in the late afternoon.

Tour Details

  • Starts & Ends: Arusha, Tanzania
  • Parks Visited: Tarangire & Ngorongoro Crater
