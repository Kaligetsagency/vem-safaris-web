8-Day Honeymoon Safari

Romance in the Bush & On the Beach

Trip Overview

Celebrate your new life together with the ultimate romantic adventure. We have carefully curated this itinerary to blend thrilling wildlife encounters in Tanzania's best parks with intimate, secluded luxury. Enjoy private candlelit dinners under the African sky, champagne picnics in the wild, and a seamless transition to a private villa on the pristine white sands of Zanzibar.

Day 1: Arrival & Romantic Welcome in Arusha

Upon arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport, your private driver will whisk you away to an exclusive, intimate boutique lodge hidden in a lush coffee plantation. Relax, unwind from your wedding and travel, and enjoy a private candlelit welcome dinner.

Day 2: Tarangire National Park & Sundowners

Your private safari guide will pick you up in a custom 4x4. We head to Tarangire, exploring the ancient baobab trees and massive elephant herds. In the late afternoon, arrive at your luxury tented camp where a private "sundowner" (cocktails and snacks) awaits you on a scenic viewpoint overlooking the wilderness.

Day 3: Ngorongoro Crater & Champagne Picnic

We drive up into the Ngorongoro Highlands and descend into the breathtaking crater. Experience unparalleled wildlife viewing as you search for the Big Five. At mid-day, your guide will set up a private, premium champagne picnic lunch away from the crowds.

Day 4: Bush Flight to Zanzibar Island

Skip the long, dusty drive back to town. After breakfast, we transfer you to the Lake Manyara airstrip for a direct, incredibly scenic bush flight to the tropical spice island of Zanzibar. A private chauffeur will meet you and transfer you to your beachfront resort on the quiet east coast.

Day 5, 6 & 7: Pristine Zanzibar Beach Retreat

Spend three full days in absolute barefoot luxury. Your ocean-facing villa is the perfect base to relax. Swim in the warm, turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean, enjoy a couples massage at the spa, or take a sunset dhow (traditional wooden boat) cruise. Dinners feature fresh, local seafood served right on the sand.

Day 8: Departure

Enjoy a final, lazy breakfast with ocean views. Depending on your flight schedule, you can do some last-minute souvenir shopping in historic Stone Town before your private transfer takes you to the airport for your journey home.

The Honeymoon Touches

  • ✓ Total Privacy: A private safari vehicle and guide—just the two of you.
  • ✓ Romantic Upgrades: Private bush dinners, sundowner cocktails, and couples spa treatments included.
  • ✓ Hot Air Balloon Option: Add a sunrise balloon safari over the wilderness for the ultimate romantic morning.
