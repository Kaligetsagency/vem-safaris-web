3-Day Zanzibar Escape

Trip Overview: The Spice Island

The perfect addition to a thrilling safari or a tough Kilimanjaro climb. Unwind on the pristine, white-sand beaches of Zanzibar, swim in the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean, and lose yourself in the rich history and exotic aromas of Stone Town.

Day 1: Arrival & Historic Stone Town

Upon arrival at Zanzibar Airport, our driver will transfer you to your hotel in Stone Town. In the afternoon, enjoy a guided walking tour through the narrow, winding streets to see the historic architecture, bustling markets, and the famous carved wooden doors.

Day 2: Spice Farm Tour & Beach Resort

After breakfast, we head to a local spice farm to discover why Zanzibar is known as the Spice Island. You will smell and taste fresh cloves, nutmeg, vanilla, and tropical fruits. Afterward, we transfer you to a beautiful beachfront resort on the coast to relax for the afternoon.

Day 3: Beach Relaxation & Departure

Enjoy a final morning soaking up the sun, walking on the beach, or taking a quick swim in the warm ocean. Depending on your flight schedule, our driver will pick you up and transfer you back to the airport for your journey home.
