1-Day Arusha National Park

Walking Safaris & Mount Meru Views

Trip Overview

Located just a short drive from Arusha city, this park is a lush, green paradise overshadowed by the towering peak of Mount Meru. It is the only park in the northern circuit where you can safely experience a thrilling walking safari accompanied by an armed park ranger, allowing you to connect with nature on foot.

Morning: Walking Safari & Tululusia Waterfall

We pick you up from your Arusha hotel at 8:00 AM. Upon entering the park, we leave the vehicle behind and join an armed park ranger for a 2-hour walking safari. Walking past grazing zebras, giraffes, and buffaloes offers a completely different perspective than a traditional game drive. The walk culminates at the beautiful Tululusia Waterfall.

Mid-Day: Momella Lakes & Canoeing (Optional)

We jump back into our 4x4 and drive toward the Momella Lakes, a series of shallow alkaline lakes famous for flocks of vibrant pink flamingos and resident hippos. You have the option to add a peaceful canoe safari here. We will enjoy a picnic lunch with views of Mount Kilimanjaro in the distance.

Late Afternoon: Ngurdoto Crater & Colobus Monkeys

In the afternoon, we drive to the Ngurdoto Crater, often called the "Little Ngorongoro." The lush forest surrounding the crater is the best place in Tanzania to spot the striking Black-and-White Colobus monkeys swinging through the canopy. We return to your Arusha hotel by 5:00 PM.
