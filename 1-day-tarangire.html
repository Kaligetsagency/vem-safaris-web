1-Day Tarangire Tour

The Elephant Capital of Northern Tanzania

Trip Overview

If you have a free day in Arusha and want to see classic African wildlife, Tarangire National Park is your best choice. Known as the "Home of the Giants," this park is famous for its ancient, towering baobab trees and some of the largest elephant herds in the world. It is highly accessible and offers phenomenal game viewing year-round.

Morning: Arusha to Tarangire

Your expert VEM Safaris guide will pick you up from your hotel in Arusha at 7:30 AM after breakfast. We embark on a comfortable 2-hour drive on a tarmac road across the Maasai Steppe. Upon arriving at the park gate, your guide will handle the paperwork while you stretch your legs, and then the adventure begins!

Mid-Day: Game Drive & River Picnic

We spend the core of the day exploring the park's diverse landscapes. During the dry season, thousands of animals migrate to the Tarangire River—the only permanent water source in the area. You can expect to see huge herds of elephants, giraffes, zebras, and wildebeest, and keep your eyes peeled for lions resting in the shade. At mid-day, we stop at a scenic picnic site overlooking the river for lunch, where you might be visited by curious velvet monkeys.

Late Afternoon: Final Game Drive & Return

After lunch, we continue our game drive through the Silale Swamps, a fantastic area for spotting leopards and pythons in the trees. In the late afternoon, we exit the park and make the drive back to Arusha, dropping you off at your hotel or the airport around 5:30 PM.
