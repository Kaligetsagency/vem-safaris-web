4-Day Classic Safari

Serengeti National Park & Ngorongoro Crater

Trip Overview

If you want to experience Tanzania's two most famous wildlife destinations but have limited time, this 4-day itinerary is perfectly crafted for you. Journey across the endless plains of the Serengeti in search of big cats, and descend into the Ngorongoro Crater to spot the endangered Black Rhino. This is the ultimate classic African safari packed into four unforgettable days.

Day 1: Arusha to Serengeti National Park

After an early breakfast in Arusha, your VEM Safaris guide will pick you up and begin the drive to the Serengeti. We pass through the lush Ngorongoro Conservation Area, enjoying spectacular views of the crater from above before descending to the vast Serengeti plains. Enjoy an en-route game drive before arriving at your camp for dinner under the stars.

Day 2: Full Day in the Serengeti

Wake up before dawn for an early morning game drive—the best time to spot predators like lions, cheetahs, and leopards while they are most active. You will spend the entire day exploring different regions of the park, tracking the resident wildlife and, depending on the season, the Great Migration herds. We will enjoy a picnic lunch in the shade of an acacia tree.

Day 3: Serengeti to Ngorongoro Crater Rim

We enjoy one final morning game drive in the Serengeti as we slowly make our way toward the park exit. In the afternoon, we drive back up into the Ngorongoro Highlands. We will reach your lodge situated right on the rim of the crater in time for a spectacular sunset and a hearty dinner.

Day 4: Ngorongoro Crater Descent to Arusha

Today we wake up very early and descend 600 meters to the crater floor. This enclosed ecosystem guarantees incredibly dense wildlife viewing. You have a very high chance of seeing all of the Big Five in a single morning! After a picnic lunch near the hippo pool, we will ascend the crater walls and make the scenic drive back to Arusha to conclude your safari.
