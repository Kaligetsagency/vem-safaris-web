5-Day Great Migration Safari

Trip Overview

Experience the raw beauty of Tanzania. This 5-day adventure takes you into the heart of the Serengeti to witness the Great Migration, followed by a descent into the world-famous Ngorongoro Crater.

Day 1: Arrival & Tarangire National Park

After a morning briefing in Arusha, we drive to Tarangire, famous for its massive elephant herds and iconic baobab trees. Enjoy an afternoon game drive before heading to your lodge.

Day 2: The Endless Plains of the Serengeti

We depart for the Serengeti, driving through the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. The afternoon is dedicated to tracking the big cats and witnessing the vast herds of the migration.

Day 3: Full Day in Serengeti National Park

A full day of exploration in the Serengeti. We will follow the wildlife trails, enjoying a picnic lunch in the bush surrounded by nature.

Day 4: Ngorongoro Crater Descent

We leave the Serengeti and descend 600 meters into the Ngorongoro Crater. This unique ecosystem is your best chance to spot the endangered Black Rhino.

Day 5: Lake Manyara to Arusha

A final morning game drive in Lake Manyara, famous for its tree-climbing lions and flamingos, before returning to Arusha for your onward journey.
